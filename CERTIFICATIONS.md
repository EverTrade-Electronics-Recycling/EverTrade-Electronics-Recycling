# Compliance & Standards — EverTrade Electronics Recycling

## Industry Standards & Compliance

EverTrade Electronics follows EPA guidelines and NIST 800-88 for responsible electronics recycling and data destruction. We do not currently hold third-party certifications such as R2, e-Stewards, NAID AAA, or ISO — see the note at the bottom of this page.

### Environmental Compliance

- **EPA Guidelines** — All recycling follows EPA recommendations for e-waste disposal
- **No Landfill Policy** — Zero electronics sent to landfills; all materials processed through responsible downstream vendors
- **Responsible Recycling** — Materials separated and processed through vetted downstream processors

### Data Security Standards

- **NIST 800-88** — All data destruction follows National Institute of Standards and Technology guidelines
- **Certificate of Destruction** — Provided for every data-bearing device processed
- **Chain of Custody** — Full documentation from pickup through final disposition

### Business Compliance Support

EverTrade's documentation helps businesses meet regulatory requirements including:

| Regulation | Industry | How We Help |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| HIPAA | Healthcare | Serialized Certificates of Destruction for PHI-bearing devices |
| SOX | Financial | Documented disposal of financial data equipment |
| GLBA | Financial Institutions | Proof of secure data destruction |
| FERPA | Education | Documented disposal of student data devices |
| PCI DSS | Retail/Payment | Documented destruction of cardholder data environments |
| TX Business Code | Texas Businesses | Compliance with state data protection laws |

## Why Certifications Matter

When choosing an electronics recycling partner, certifications and documentation matter:

1. **Legal Protection** — Proper documentation protects your company from liability
2. **Regulatory Compliance** — Many industries require documented disposal of IT equipment
3. **Environmental Responsibility** — Responsible recyclers ensure no e-waste enters landfills
4. **Data Security** — Verified data destruction prevents data breaches and identity theft

EverTrade operates under EPA and NIST 800-88 guidelines and provides serialized documentation for every device. Customers whose procurement requires a specific third-party certification (R2, e-Stewards, NAID AAA, ISO) should contact us to confirm fit — for many use cases our documentation plus vetted downstream processors satisfies audit requirements, but we'll tell you plainly if it doesn't.
