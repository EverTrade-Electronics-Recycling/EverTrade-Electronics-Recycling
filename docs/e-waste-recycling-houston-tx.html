Why Responsible E-Waste Matters

  • Legal compliance — Texas and federal regulations require proper e-waste disposal for businesses
  • Data security — Improperly discarded electronics expose sensitive business and customer data
  • Environmental responsibility — E-waste contains toxic materials (lead, mercury, cadmium) that contaminate soil and water
  • Brand protection — Dumped electronics with your company's data or branding can be a PR disaster
  • Revenue recovery — Resalable equipment can offset disposal costs through our ITAD program

How It Works

  • 4. Processing — Equipment is sorted for resale, recycling, or destruction.
