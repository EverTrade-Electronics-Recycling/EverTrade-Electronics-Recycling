Environmental Compliance

  • EPA-aligned e-waste handling — Recycling follows EPA guidance for electronic waste
  • No-landfill policy — Electronics are not landfilled; materials flow through vetted downstream processors
  • Responsible downstream vendors — Relationships with processors that specialize in CRT glass, batteries, and precious-metal recovery

Data Security Standards

  • NIST 800-88 — Data destruction follows National Institute of Standards and Technology guidelines (Clear / Purge / Destroy)
  • Certificate of Destruction — Issued for every data-bearing device, with serial number and method recorded
  • Chain of custody — Full documentation from pickup through final disposition

Why Documentation Matters

  1. Legal protection — Proper documentation protects your company from liability
  2. Regulatory compliance — Many industries require documented disposal of IT equipment
  3. Environmental responsibility — Documented downstream flow prevents e-waste from entering landfills
  4. Data security — Verified destruction helps prevent data breaches and identity theft
A note on third-party certifications: EverTrade operates under EPA and NIST 800-88 guidelines and provides serialized documentation for every device. Customers whose procurement requires a specific third-party certification (R2, e-Stewards, NAID AAA, ISO) should contact us to confirm fit — for many use cases our documentation plus vetted downstream processors satisfies audit requirements, but we'll tell you plainly if it doesn't.
