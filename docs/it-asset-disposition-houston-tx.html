What is IT Asset Disposition?

IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) is the process of responsibly managing retired IT equipment — from decommissioning through data destruction, resale, or recycling. The goal: maximize the value you recover from retired assets while ensuring data security and regulatory compliance.

EverTrade handles the entire ITAD lifecycle for Houston businesses, from pickup to final disposition.

The ITAD Process

1. Assessment

Inventory and evaluate your retired equipment for resale or recycling potential.

2. Data Destruction

NIST 800-88 compliant wiping, degaussing, or shredding. Certificate issued per device.

3. Value Recovery

Resalable equipment is refurbished and sold, with revenue shared back to you.

4. Responsible Recycling

Non-resalable equipment is recycled responsibly. Zero landfill.

5. Documentation

Full chain-of-custody records, certificates of destruction and recycling.

6. Compliance Reporting

Audit-ready reports for HIPAA, SOX, GLBA, and PCI compliance.
