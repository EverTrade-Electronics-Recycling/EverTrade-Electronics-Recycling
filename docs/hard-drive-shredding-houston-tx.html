Why Hard Drive Shredding Matters

Simply deleting files or reformatting a drive doesn't erase your data. Forensic recovery tools can retrieve information from "wiped" drives in minutes. Physical destruction — shredding your hard drives into fragments — is the only guaranteed way to ensure your data is permanently unrecoverable.
