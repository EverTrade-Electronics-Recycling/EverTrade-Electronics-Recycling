Three Tiers of Data Destruction

🧹 Software Wiping (Clear)

NIST 800-88 Clear method. Overwrite all addressable locations. Ideal for drives being reused or resold.

🧲 Degaussing (Purge)

Magnetic field destroys all data. Drive becomes permanently inoperable. Best for high-security needs.

🔩 Physical Shredding (Destroy)

Drives physically shredded into fragments. The most secure option. Witness destruction or get video proof.
