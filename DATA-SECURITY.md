# Data Security — EverTrade Electronics Recycling

> Your data security is our top priority. EverTrade follows NIST 800-88 guidelines and provides complete documentation for every device we process.

## Data Destruction Methods

### Software-Based Sanitization (NIST 800-88 Clear)
- Multi-pass overwrite of all data-bearing devices
- Suitable for devices being resold or redeployed
- Verification pass confirms complete data removal
- Certificate of Sanitization provided

### Degaussing (NIST 800-88 Purge)
- Electromagnetic degaussing destroys all magnetic data
- Renders hard drives completely unreadable
- Appropriate for HIPAA, SOX, and other compliance requirements
- Drive cannot be reused after degaussing

### Physical Destruction (NIST 800-88 Destroy)
- Industrial shredding of hard drives and SSDs
- On-site and off-site options available
- Metal fragments recycled through R2-certified downstream
- Certificate of Destruction with serial number documentation

## Chain of Custody

Every device tracked from pickup through final disposition:

1. **Intake** — Equipment cataloged with asset tags and serial numbers
2. **Segregation** — Data-bearing devices separated and secured
3. **Data Destruction** — Chosen method applied per your requirements
4. **Documentation** — Photos, serial numbers, and method recorded
5. **Certificate** — Official Certificate of Destruction or Sanitization issued
6. **Final Disposition** — Equipment recycled, resold, or disposed of per your instructions
